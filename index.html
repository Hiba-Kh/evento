About Us

This website is developed as a graduation project by 2 student from an-Najah National University, the computer engineering department, Hiba Khalifah and Shahira Arafat.

What is Our Goal?

We create this website to help anyone who wants to create and manage his\her conference, by developing a full interactive conference plan up system that provides these features:

  • Conference creation, announcing, locating, scheduling, and modifying.
  • Scientific studies, papers, proceedings publishing.
  • Keep track of all events that you want to attend or just interested in, in you profile page.
  • Analysis system to evaluate your conference and how successful it was
  • Give the concurrent and live activities and sessions in the conference in the presenting time by a related mobile application .
  • Create a virtual society by a group chat system, to make all audience in a specific conference in touch.
